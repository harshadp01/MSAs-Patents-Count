Regional Trends

Highest Activity Areas: The biggest number of patents is found in coastal areas, especially in California and the Northeast.

Moderate Activity Areas: LA and New York significantly contribute to patent counts.

Low Activity Areas: Boston regions show lower patent counts compared to other coastal MSAs.
